# Dev Log

> This is your development journal. After each session with Claude Code, a log entry will be added here documenting what you worked on, what you learned, and what was confusing.
>
> **How it works:** At the end of each session, Claude Code will draft an entry for you. Your job is to read it, edit it in your own words, and make sure it makes sense to *you*. Writing about what you learned is one of the best ways to make it stick.
